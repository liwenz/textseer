Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 1, 2024

Welcome to Text Recognizer App (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We highly value your privacy protection. This policy details how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection and Use

As an offline text recognition tool, this App follows the principle of minimal data collection:

  • Camera Data: Used for real-time text recognition and barcode scanning. All image processing is done locally on your device, and image data never leaves your device.
  • Image Files: When you select photos from your gallery for recognition, images are temporarily processed in device memory only and are not uploaded to any servers.
  • Recognition Results: Extracted text and barcode information is temporarily stored in device memory during app runtime only, for your copy, share and other operations.
  • App Settings: Your personal preference settings (such as language selection) are stored locally on the device only.

2. Third-Party Services

This App uses Google ML Kit for text and barcode recognition functions. According to Google's privacy policy, certain recognition processes may involve data transmission. For details, please refer to: Google Privacy Policy

3. Permission Explanation

This App requires the following permissions to achieve core functions:

  • Camera Permission: Used only for real-time scanning of text and barcodes, not for video recording or surveillance.
  • Gallery Permission: Used only for selecting image files to be recognized.
  • Network Permission: Used for potential model updates required by ML Kit.

4. Data Storage and Retention

All data processing is completed on your device:

  • Recognition results are not stored on our servers
  • Image data is immediately cleared from memory after processing
  • App settings are saved only in your device's local storage

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data processing occurs on the device
  • No personal identification information is collected
  • User data is not shared with other apps or services

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and notified to you through in-app notifications.
